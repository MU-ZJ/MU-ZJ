Potato Salad


Ingredients

  • 5 pounds of potatoes.
  • 2 dozen eggs.
  • 1 large green pepper.
  • 1 teaspoon of finely diced onion.
  • 1 tablespoon of sweet relish.
  • 1 jar of mayonaise.
  • 4 tablespoon of dijonaisse.
  • 6 stalks of celery.
  • Vinegar, to taste.
  • Sugar, to taste.
  • Salt, to taste.
  • Celery seed, to taste.

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes in salted water, let cook completely.
  2. Hard boil eggs, peel and let cool completely.
  3. Peel and dice potatoes.
  4. Save 8 egg yolks, and dice remaining eggs.
  5. Chop celery, pepper, and onion. Add to potatoes.
  6. Add relish.
  7. Make a sauce, combining egg yolks, dijonaisse, mayonaise, salt, sugar, vinegar, and celery seed to taste.
  8. Pour sauce over potaotes and mix.
  9. Add salt to taste.
