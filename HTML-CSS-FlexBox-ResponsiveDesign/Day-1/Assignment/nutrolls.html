Nut Rolls


Ingredients

  • 1 pound of butter.
  • 8 ounces of cream cheese.
  • 6 cups of flour.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt.
  • 5 egg yolks.
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract.
  • 1 packet of dry yeast.
  • 1/3 cup of water.

Instructions

  1. Mix water and cream cheese.
  2. Put yeast in water and let rise.
  3. Mix flour and salt.
  4. Add butter mixture and work like pie dough.
  5. Mix egg yolks and vanilla.
  6. Divide into 16 even balls, flatten and put wax paper between layers.
  7. Refridgerate overnigh.
  8. Roll layers in powdered sugar.
  9. Spread with nut mixture and cut into 8-10 small rolls.
  10. Bake at 350f for 18-20 minutes.
