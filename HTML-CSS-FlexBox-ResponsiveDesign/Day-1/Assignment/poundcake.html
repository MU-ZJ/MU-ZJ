Pound Cake


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of sugar.
  • 2 cups of butter.
  • 4 cups of flour.
  • 10 eggs.
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla.

Instructions

  1. Beat egg whites and yolks seperately.
  2. Cream butter, then cream sugar into it.
  3. Add egg yolks and mix well.
  4. Add stiff egg whites alternatively with flour.
  5. Put into oven while still cool enough not to burn hand.
  6. Gradually increase temperature to 350f, and bake for 2 hours.
Note: Ensure mixture is sufficiently beaten before baking. This recipe does not use yeast or baking soda, and must be leavened by hand.
