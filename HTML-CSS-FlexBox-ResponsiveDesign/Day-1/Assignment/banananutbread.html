Banana Nut Bread


Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of mashed bananas.
  • 1/3 cup of vegetable shortening.
  • 2 eggs.
  • 1 and 3/4 cups of flour.
  • 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda.
  • 1/5 teaspoon of salt.
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder.

    • Instructions

    1. Sift dry ingredients.
    2. Beat vegetetable shortening and sugar until creamed.
    3. Add eggs and beat well.
    4. Add flour and banana alternately.
    5. Bake at 350f for 30 minutes.
