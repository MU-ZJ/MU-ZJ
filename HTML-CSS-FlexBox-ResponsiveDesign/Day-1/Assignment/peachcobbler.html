Peach Cobbler


Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 pints of peaches.
  • 1/2 cup of sugar.
  • 1 cup of fresh buttermilk.
  • 1/2 cup of butter.
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda.
  • 1 pinch of salt.
  • 2 cups (approx.) of flour.

Instructions

  1. Butter a 2 quart pudding dish and add 1 and 1/2 pints of peeled and pitted peaches.
  2. Cover with water and add 1/2 cup of sugar.
  3. Make a crust of 1 cup fresh buttermilk, 1/2 cup of butter or meat drippings, and 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda with a pinch of salt.
  4. Mix in flour, and roll dough to fit pudding dish.
  5. Place crust over peaches, and score crust.
  6. Bake until peaches and crust are well done (temperature not specified in recipe).
  7. Serve hot, either with or without sauce.
